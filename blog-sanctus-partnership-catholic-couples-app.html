AIBridges Partners with Sanctus: Building Technology for Catholic Couples

January 30, 2026 4 min read Partnerships

Sanctus

We're thrilled to announce that AIBridges is now partnering with Sanctus, a beautiful faith-based application designed to help Catholic couples strengthen their marriages through daily devotions, relationship tools, and spiritual growth.

This partnership represents exactly the kind of work we love at AIBridges: using technology to serve meaningful purposes and bring people closer together - in this case, both to each other and to their faith.

Our Mission Alignment: At AIBridges, we believe technology should serve human flourishing. Sanctus embodies this principle by providing couples with tools for deeper connection, communication, and shared spiritual growth.

What is Sanctus?

Sanctus is a mobile application built specifically for Catholic couples - whether dating, engaged, or married. In a world full of apps designed to distract and divide, Sanctus is designed to unite couples in faith and love.

The app provides:

Daily Devotions

Scripture readings and reflections designed for couples to share together each day.

Conversation Starters

Thoughtful prompts to spark meaningful discussions about faith, life, and your relationship.

Relationship Tools

Practical exercises for communication, conflict resolution, and growing closer together.

Liturgical Calendar

Stay connected to the rhythm of the Church year with feast days, saints, and seasonal devotions.

Why This Partnership Matters

Catholic couples face unique challenges in today's world. Between demanding careers, raising families, and the constant pull of digital distractions, it's easy for spiritual life - both individual and shared - to fall by the wayside.

Sanctus addresses this by meeting couples where they are: on their phones. Instead of competing with social media for attention, it offers something life-giving in the same accessible format.

Technology in Service of the Sacred

We've seen how technology can erode relationships and fragment attention. But it doesn't have to be that way. The same tools that can distract can also be used to:

  • Create daily rituals of connection
  • Provide structure for spiritual practice
  • Remind couples of what matters most
  • Build habits that strengthen marriages over time

That's the vision behind Sanctus, and it's why we're proud to support their mission.

Get Started with Sanctus

Ready to bring more faith and intentionality into your relationship? Getting started with Sanctus is simple:

  1. Visit Sanctus on our site
  2. Create your account with email or Google
  3. Share your invite code with your partner
  4. Start your first devotion together

The app is designed to take just a few minutes each day - enough to create meaningful connection without adding another burden to busy lives.

Start Your Journey Together

Whether you're newly dating or celebrating decades of marriage, Sanctus meets you where you are and helps you grow together in faith.
