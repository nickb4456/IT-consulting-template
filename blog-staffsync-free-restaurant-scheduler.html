StaffSync hits the sweet spot: simple enough to learn in minutes, powerful enough to actually solve your scheduling problems.

How Shift Selling Works

Here's the workflow that restaurant staff actually love:

  1. Employee posts a shift: "I need someone to cover my Saturday 5-10pm server shift"
  2. Qualified staff see it: Only employees with the right role and availability see the open shift
  3. Someone picks it up: Another server claims the shift
  4. Manager approves: You get a notification and approve (or deny) with one tap
  5. Schedule updates: Everyone sees the change immediately

The whole process takes minutes instead of the usual back-and-forth that can drag on for days.
