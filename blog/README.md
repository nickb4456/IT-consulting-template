## SEO Strategy

### Content Features
- **Data-driven:** Every article cites real settlement data from CaseVault's 1,756-case database
- **Long-form:** 1,700-2,500 words per article (ideal for SEO)
- **Structured:** Headers, tables, bullet points for scannability
- **CTA:** Each article includes call-to-action to sign up for CaseVault trial
- **Internal linking:** Cross-reference between articles where relevant

### Target Audience
1. **Primary:** Injury victims googling "what is my case worth" or "average settlement for [injury]"
2. **Secondary:** PI attorneys researching settlement data for case valuation

### Optimization Notes
- Titles optimized for Google featured snippets
- Meta descriptions under 160 characters
- Keywords in H1, first paragraph, and naturally throughout
- Tables and lists for featured snippet capture
- FAQ sections for voice search optimization
