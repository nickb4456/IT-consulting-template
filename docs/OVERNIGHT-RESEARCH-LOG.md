#### Executive Summary

Integration with practice management systems (PMS) is the **highest-leverage go-to-market channel** for DraftBridge. Clio alone has 150,000+ legal professionals and 250-300 apps in their directory. Getting listed in these marketplaces creates:
- Built-in distribution to warm leads
- Credibility through association
- Referral revenue from platform sales teams
- Reduced customer acquisition costs

#### Key Findings

##### 1. CLIO INTEGRATION ECOSYSTEM (Priority #1)

**Scale & Opportunity:**
- 150,000+ legal professionals on platform
- 250-302 apps in App Directory (legal industry's largest ecosystem)
- 37 pages of integrations
- Exposure to over 150,000 tech-forward legal professionals

**Certified App Program (Two Tiers):**

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Silver** | 100+ active Clio customer accounts, Implement "Add to Clio", 99.5% uptime, 1-day support SLA, Security review passed | Partnership manager, Certified badge, Sales team recommends you, Early API access |
| **Gold** | Invitation-only, All Silver reqs + deep integration | Dedicated Slack channel, Custom go-to-market plan, Joint case studies, Present to Clio sales team |

**Onboarding Process:**
1. Build integration using Clio API
2. Complete security/compliance questionnaire via Securiti
3. Submit pre-recorded demo to api.partnerships@clio.com
4. Create App Directory listing
5. Launch with Clio support

**Security Requirements (Critical):**
- HTTPS required (aim for A+ on SSL Labs)
- OWASP top 10 compliance
- Public Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Security Policy
- Data residency compliance (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA)
- SOC2/ISO27001 certification helpful but not required
- Salted password hashes (bcrypt recommended)

**Integration Awards Program:**
- Annual awards at ClioCon (2025 deadline: June 27)
- Categories: Best New App, Best Business of Law, Best Practice of Law
- Winners receive: Pitch coaching with Clio Ventures, ClioCon booth, airfare/hotel, funding consideration
- 2025 winners: VERA (deadlines), CollBox (collections), Clearbrief (legal briefs)

**Contact:** api.partnerships@clio.com

##### 7. ZAPIER AS A BRIDGE STRATEGY

**Why Start Here:**
- Quick path to integration (vs. native API development)
- Connects DraftBridge to 1,000+ apps
- Already supported by: Clio, MyCase, Smokeball, Actionstep, PracticePanther

**Use Cases:**
- Auto-create DraftBridge documents when Clio matter created

- Trigger workflows based on document completion

**Recommendation:** Build Zapier integration FIRST, then pursue native integrations with validated customer demand.
