FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI solutions and services.

What is an AI agent and how can it help my business? +

An AI agent is software that handles repetitive tasks autonomously — email triage, document processing, data entry, scheduling. It works 24/7, follows your rules, and frees your team to focus on higher-value work. We customize agents to fit your existing workflows.

How much does AI integration cost for a small business? +

Our discovery call is free. Agent builds start at $2,500 for a custom deployment, and ongoing managed service (the Agentic Office retainer) starts at $1,500/month with usage caps so costs stay predictable.

Is my data safe with local AI models? +

Yes. We deploy models that run entirely on your own hardware — no cloud APIs, no third-party data sharing. For law firms, medical practices, and financial advisors, this means privileged data never leaves your building.

Do you work with law firms? +

Absolutely. Legal tech is one of our core specialties. We've built SwiftShot for evidence capture, CaseVault for settlement data, document processing tools, and private AI deployments specifically for law firms handling sensitive client files.

What's included in the Agentic Office retainer? +

The retainer includes 24/7 agent monitoring, monthly performance reports, usage-capped API costs, prompt and workflow improvements, new agent builds as needs arise, and direct access to the engineer managing your system.

Do you serve businesses outside Rhode Island? +

Yes. While we're based in Cranston, RI and most of our clients are in Rhode Island and New England, we work with businesses across the US remotely. On-site deployment is available in the greater Providence and Boston areas.
