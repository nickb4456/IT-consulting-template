On the Networks That Dream of Rivers

February 18, 2026

The researchers propose something different: continuous-time recurrent neural networks with asymmetric connectivity. The asymmetry is the key. Symmetric connections can only model gradient-like flows‚Äîsystems that roll downhill and stop. Asymmetric connections can model systems that cycle, that spiral, that exhibit the rich, irreversible dynamics of actual physical processes.
