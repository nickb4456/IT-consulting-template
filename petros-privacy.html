Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Petros ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in Petros.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Petros is designed for children under 13 years of age. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Petros does not collect, store, or share:

  • Names or usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Photos or videos
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Any personally identifiable information

Local Data Storage

Petros stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Progress in faith activities
  • Saved prayers and bookmarks

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

No Advertising

Petros does not display any advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking technologies.

No Third-Party Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services that track user behavior. We do not share any data with analytics providers.

No External Links

Petros does not contain links to external websites or apps. All content is contained within the app to ensure a safe experience for children.

In-App Purchases

If Petros offers in-app purchases, they are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain or delete. Local app data can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

Parental Rights

Parents can:

  • Review the app content at any time
  • Delete the app and all local data from the device
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
