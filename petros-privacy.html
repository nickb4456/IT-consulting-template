Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Kephas ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Kephas is designed with children's privacy as a priority:

  • No account creation required
  • No personal data collected
  • No location tracking
  • No analytics or tracking cookies
  • No third-party advertising

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Prayer streak and meditation history
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kephas is designed for use by children under 13 with parental supervision. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by:

  • Not collecting personal information from children
  • Not requiring registration or accounts
  • Not including behavioral advertising
  • Not including links to external websites or apps

Third-Party Services

Kephas does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline after download.

In-App Purchases

Kephas contains no in-app purchases. All content is included with the initial download.

Advertisements

Kephas contains no advertisements of any kind.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your family's information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
