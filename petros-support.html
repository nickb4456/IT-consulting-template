Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Petros?

Petros is available on the Apple App Store. Search for "Petros" or visit our app page for a direct link.

Is Petros free?

Petros offers free content with optional premium features available through a subscription.

What age is Petros designed for?

Petros is designed for children ages 4-12, with content appropriate for Catholic families.

Does Petros require an internet connection?

Most features work offline after initial download. Some content like the daily saint may require an internet connection to update.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. Petros does not collect any personal information. All data stays on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open Petros on your new device and go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

The app is crashing or not working properly.

Try these steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store, 2) Restart the app, 3) Restart your device, 4) Reinstall the app. If problems persist, contact us.
