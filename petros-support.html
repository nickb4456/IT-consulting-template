Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kephas free?

Yes! Kephas is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions. All content is included with the download.

What ages is Kephas designed for?

Kephas is designed for children ages 4-12, though younger children may enjoy it with parental guidance. The games and prayers are accessible for various reading levels.

Does Kephas work offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, Kephas works completely offline. No internet connection is required to play games, pray, or explore saint stories.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Reset Progress." This will clear all game scores and prayer streaks. Note: this cannot be undone.

Is my child's data collected?

No. Kephas collects zero personal data. All progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Will there be more games added?

Yes! We're actively developing new Bible-based games and features. Updates are delivered automatically through the App Store.

Can I suggest a feature or saint?

Absolutely! Email us at support@aibridges.org with your ideas. We love hearing from families.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Restart your device, 3) Check for app updates in the App Store, 4) If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

Audio isn't playing

Make sure your device isn't in silent mode and the volume is turned up. Check that Kephas has permission to play audio in your device settings.

My progress was lost

Progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, or got a new device, previous progress cannot be recovered. We're working on optional cloud backup for a future update.
