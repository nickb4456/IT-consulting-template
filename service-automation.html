Automation Success Stories

1

Automated Invoice Processing

A contractor was spending 3 hours every Friday typing job completion data into QuickBooks to create invoices. We automated it: when a job is marked complete in their scheduling app, an invoice is automatically created and emailed to the customer. Friday afternoons are now free.

2

Lead Follow-Up Sequences

A real estate agent was losing leads because follow-up was inconsistent. We built an automation that sends a personalized text and email immediately when someone submits a form, then follows up at day 3, 7, and 14 if they don't respond. Lead conversion increased 25%.

3

Inventory Alerts & Reordering

A boutique shop kept running out of popular items. We connected their POS to a simple alert system that notifies the owner when inventory drops below set levels, and automatically creates a purchase order draft for their main supplier. No more surprise stock-outs.
