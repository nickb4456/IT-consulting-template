Real Projects

Infrastructure Solutions in Action

1

Cloud Migration Done Right

A law firm's aging server was failing. We migrated them to Microsoft 365 with zero downtime — 15 users, 2TB of files, and decades of email archives moved over a weekend. They now access everything from anywhere, and we eliminated their $400/month server maintenance costs.

2

Bulletproof Backups

A medical practice needed HIPAA-compliant backups. We implemented automated daily backups to encrypted cloud storage, with monthly recovery tests. When ransomware hit a neighboring practice, our client had peace of mind knowing they could recover in hours, not weeks.

3

Network Security Overhaul

A retail business was using consumer-grade routers and no firewall. We installed business-class networking with proper segmentation (POS systems isolated from guest WiFi), intrusion detection, and VPN access for the owner. They passed their next PCI compliance audit with flying colors.
