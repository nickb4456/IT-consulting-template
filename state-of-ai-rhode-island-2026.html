Industry Breakdown

Legal (n=8)

Law firms show the highest custom AI adoption at 38%. Primary use cases: document review, legal research, and client intake automation. Privacy concerns drive preference for local/private AI deployments â€” 75% of firms surveyed prefer on-premise solutions over cloud AI.

Healthcare (n=7)

HIPAA compliance remains the primary barrier. Only 29% have adopted AI tools, but those who have report the highest satisfaction scores (4.6/5). Main applications: appointment scheduling, medical transcription, and patient communication.

Retail & Restaurants (n=15)

Highest adoption rate at 73%, driven by inventory management and scheduling tools. Average time savings of 22 hours/month per location. StaffSync and similar shift-management AI tools see particularly strong uptake.

Professional Services (n=12)

Accountants, consultants, and agencies report 58% adoption. Document automation leads (client proposals, reports), followed by email triage. Concerns about AI accuracy in client-facing communications limit broader deployment.
