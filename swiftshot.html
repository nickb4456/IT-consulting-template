Evidence That Holds Up in Court

Three steps from capture to court-defensible evidence

1

Capture & Hash

Every screenshot is immediately SHA-256 hashed at the moment of capture. The hash proves the image hasn't been altered since it was taken.

2

Preserve to WORM

One click uploads to AWS S3 Object Lock storage. Governance mode prevents deletion or modification during your retention period — even by admins.

3

Chain of Custody

Every action is logged: who captured it, when, from which URL, the hash, and when it was preserved. A complete audit trail for opposing counsel.
